TOUR MITRA

Introduction

Tour Mitra is a comprehensive web-based application designed to enhance the travel experience for tourists by providing two major functionalities: sentiment analysis and Tour place information. The platform aims to assist travelers in making informed decisions by analyzing public sentiment for popular tourist destinations and displaying specific, curated information about places, including hours of operation, entrance fees, and best visit times.

Objective

The primary objective of Tour Mitra is to offer a reliable, AI-driven tool for evaluating the sentiment associated with various tourist attractions and to give useful, contextual details about these locations. By combining advanced NLP techniques with comprehensive location data, Tour Mitra empowers users to gain a deeper understanding of the places they plan to visit.

Development Phase

The sentiment analysis module utilizes two models:

  • VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary and sEntiment Reasoner) - Optimal for capturing nuances in shorter text reviews
  • RoBERTa (Transformer-based model) - Handles more complex language patterns

The Natural Language Toolkit (NLTK) is employed for essential preprocessing tasks such as:

  • Tokenization
  • Removal of stop words
  • Part-of-speech tagging

Requirements

  • Languages: Python, HTML, JavaScript
  • Libraries: NLTK, Pandas, Flask, JSON handling libraries
  • Models: VADER and RoBERTa
  • Front-end: Responsive HTML, CSS

Conclusion

Tour Mitra is an AI-enabled platform that bridges the gap between tourists and location-based insights, providing a one-stop solution for sentiment and information analysis of travel destinations. The project sets a foundation for expanding into further personalized travel recommendations.
